A study permit application should show more than an admission letter. Officers also look for financial readiness, a credible study plan, and a clear explanation of how the program fits your education and career goals.

Students can prepare by collecting school acceptance documents, tuition information, proof of funds, identity documents, and a statement explaining why the program and location make sense.

## Common Gaps

Applications often become weaker when funds are unclear, documents are inconsistent, or the study plan is too generic. These issues can usually be improved before submission with careful review.
