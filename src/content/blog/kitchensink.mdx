An immigration consultation is a focused conversation about your background, goals, timeline, and available pathways. It is also a chance to understand what documents may be needed before committing to a full application.

## Before the Appointment

Bring or prepare key dates, passport details, previous immigration history, employment or education records, and any refusal letters or IRCC correspondence if applicable.

## During the Consultation

The consultant will ask questions, identify possible pathways, explain risks, and outline next steps. For many clients, the most valuable outcome is knowing what to do first and what to avoid.

## After the Consultation

You should leave with a practical checklist and a clearer sense of whether you need application review, full representation, or more time to collect documents.
