A visitor visa application should make the reason for travel easy to understand. Whether the visit is for family, tourism, or a special event, the file should connect the purpose of travel with dates, funds, and plans to leave Canada at the end of the stay.

Helpful evidence may include a travel itinerary, invitation letter, proof of funds, employment or business ties, family ties, and previous travel history.

## Keep the Story Consistent

Dates, names, and financial details should match across the application. Small inconsistencies can create unnecessary questions, especially when the file is otherwise straightforward.

## Before You Submit

A review can help identify missing documents, unclear explanations, or evidence that should be reorganized before the application is sent.
